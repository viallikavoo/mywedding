Who we are?

Who we are?

While not everyone have the meet-cute that we all see in the movies, but we all need a special reminder that magical things still happen.. all the time! Vialli and Fleur have crossed paths many times before they met. Were opposites from the start but love happens in the most surprising of ways. Being introduced to each other by family and friends, Vialli & Fleur became the closest of friends themselves. Little did they know destiny was already devising a plan to make this match happen. After a while of getting to know each other and a few clumsy moments later by some miracle they started dating. 6 years worth of memories later, they've backpacked a few countries, moved in together, and discovered a mutual love for traveling, gaming and food!
